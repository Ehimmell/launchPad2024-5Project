Common Q&A

How do you get your data and summaries?

For our summaries, we gather reviews on each professor on rateMyProfessors. Then, we feed these reviews to Generative AI to create a summary of them all.

For our statistics, we gather data from Purdue's course catalog and grade distributions. We also gather information like average GPA in a class through BoilerGrades.

What information do you provide about a professor?

Currently, we provide a summary of reviews on a professor, the average GPA in their class, and whether or not it's likely that they will improve next semester based on reviews through time.
